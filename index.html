Common Questions

Do you accept cash?

✅ Yes, we accept cash, debit, credit cards and e-transfer. We offer multiple payment options for your convenience.

Will it take long to get my clothes back?

❌ No, you will get your clothes back within 24-48 hours maximum (except weekends). We offer fast turnaround times for all orders.

Do you separate whites, colors, and special fabrics?

✅ Yes, we separate whites, darks and colors and also delicate items. We can also hand wash for an additional charge upon request.

Do you charge for pick ups and deliveries?

❌ No, we offer free pick up and free delivery for all our laundry services in Ottawa and Gatineau.

Do we need to be home for pick up and delivery?

❌ No, you can leave your laundry in front of the door and we will take care of it. We provide secure pickup and delivery even when you're not home.

What if I have special instructions?

✅ We accommodate special requests! Just let us know in the booking form or contact us directly. We're here to make your laundry experience perfect.
